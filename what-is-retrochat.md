# What is RetroChat?

RetroChat is a modern, decentralized social platform that combines the nostalgia of retro chat rooms with cutting-edge Web3 technology. Built on Solana, RetroChat offers a unique blend of community features, gaming, and blockchain integration.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Chat**: Public rooms and direct messaging
- **Arcade Games**: Classic games integrated into the platform
- **Radio Streaming**: Browse and create music playlists
- **Web3 Integration**: Solana wallet support, tipping, and airdrops
- **User Profiles**: Customizable profiles with follow system
- **Tokenomics**: Native token with utility and staking (coming soon)

## Why RetroChat?

RetroChat brings together the best of both worlds:
- The community feel of early internet chat rooms
- Modern Web3 capabilities and token economics
- A platform that rewards active community members
